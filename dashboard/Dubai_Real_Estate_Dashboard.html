📅 March Surge
March 2023 saw 16,446 transactions — the highest volume month. Likely Q1 end-of-quarter activity and new project launches.
📉 April Dip + Recovery
April dropped to 10,679 transactions but price held at 14,975 AED/sqm — proving demand is inelastic even when volume falls.
📈 Rising Price Trend
Despite volume fluctuating, avg price rose from 15,142 to 16,087 AED/sqm Jan→Jun — clear upward price trajectory.
